### WorldGen Generation Details (from WorldGen.cs)

**Surface Jungle:**
- MudCavesToJungleGrass pass: SpreadGrass converts mud (59) → jungle grass (60)
- GlowingMushroomPlantsUndergroundAndJunglePlants pass: places tile 61 on tile 60
- JunglePlantsPart2 pass: places tile 233 (PlantDetritus, 2x2) on jungle grass

**Underground Jungle:**
- UndergroundJungleTrees pass: generates jungle trees (tile 5, TreeType.Jungle)
- JungleShrines pass: structures with chests, gold brick (45), torches
- Vines pass: generates hanging vines (62) from jungle grass, 1-10 tile chains
- Hive generation: tiles 225 (Hive), 226 (HoneyBlock), 229 (CrispyHoney), 231 (Larva)
- Bee Hive decorations: tile 444 (BeeHive, 2x2 hanging)

**Hardmode:**
- Life Fruit (236), Plantera Bulb (238) spawn in underground jungle
- Chlorophyte ore (211) grows from mud/jungle grass
- Corrupt/Crimson jungle grass variants (661, 662)

### WorldGen Generation Details (from WorldGen.cs)

**ShroomPatch() method (lines 62028-62111):**
- Creates mud sphere (59) + mushroom wall (80) in underground
- Inner 20% radius: clears tiles, sets wall 80
- Outer 40% radius: places mud, sets wall 80
- SpreadGrass converts mud (59) → mushroom grass (70)
- 1 patch per ~700 tiles of world width

**GlowingMushroomPlantsUndergroundAndJunglePlants pass:**
- Places mushroom plants (71) on mushroom grass (70), up to 3 attempts per spot
- Grows giant mushroom trees (72) via TryGrowingTreeByType

**Vines pass:**
- Places mushroom vines (528) hanging from mushroom grass
- 20% spawn rate per grass tile, 1-10 tile chains

**Biome conversion (Type 3):**
- Jungle grass (60) → Mushroom grass (70)
- Walls in CanBeConvertedToGlowingMushroom set (64,67,15,247) → 80
- Thorns → KillTile
- Torches → mushroom style 22
